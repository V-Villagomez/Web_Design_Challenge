Gender Stats


The Gender Stats shows the comparison between females vs. males who walk the Camino each year from 2004 through 2018. Data shows that over the years, males continue to be the highest number of pilgrims who walk the Camino. However, over the years, we can also see that the percentages of females walking the Camino is increasing. In 2004, 55.81% of males walked the Camino compared to 44.19% of females. In comparion, 14 years later in 2018, 50.35% of males walked the Camino and 49.65% of females also walked the Camino.
