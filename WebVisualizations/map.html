Map Stats


The Map Stats shows the comaparison of the number of pilgrims per country around the world who walked the Camino from 2004 through 2018. For this data set, the number of pilgrims from Spain needed to be removed becuase the majority of pilgrims were from Spain. Thus, by removing pilgrims from Spain, we were able to see a better representation of the number of pilgrims around the world. By looking at the Map, we can see that the majority of pilgrims were from Europe as opposed to other contients.
